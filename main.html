The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero crime thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in Gotham City following Batman Begins, the film sees Batman (Christian Bale), Lt. Jim Gordon, and District Attorney Harvey Dent join forces to take down organized crime. Their efforts unravel as the Joker (Heath Ledger), a chaotic criminal mastermind, emerges to challenge Batman’s moral boundaries and reshape Gotham’s fate
